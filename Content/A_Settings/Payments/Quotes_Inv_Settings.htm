Quotes and Invoices Settings

This is where you set up your payment terms, preferred payment methods, business address, and the terms and conditions related to quotes and invoices.

hipages tradiecore facilitates the following 2 methods of receiving payments from customers:

Digital Payments

Digital Payments in hipages tradiecore are facilitated by hipages payments. hipages Payments is a convenient payment solution for you to get paid faster when you send quotes and invoices. Powered by Stripe, you now have,

  • A secure way of accepting payments from any customer. You can accept credit card payments (Visa, Mastercard or AMEX) along with Apple Pay, Google Pay, and coming soon to Apple devices, (Beta) Tap to Pay.

  • An easy-to-use payment platform.

  • A leading secure digital payments infrastructure.

  • Automatically add a payment link to customers email invoices.

To find out how you can set up hipages payments, see Connecting to hipages Payments.

You will receive funds within 2 business days to the bank account that you nominated, when setting up hipages payments or your Stripe account.

Bank Transfers

If you do not have digital payment methods set up for your business, you can choose the direct bank transfer option to receive payments from your customers.

You will need to enter your banking information such as BSB and account number into hipages tradiecore. These details will then be included in all the PDF invoices and quotes that you send to your customers.

For information on how to set your banking details, see Setting up bank details and transfers.

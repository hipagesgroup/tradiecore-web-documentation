Tap to Pay

Introducing Tap to Pay in hipages tradiecore. You can now effortlessly accept payments directly from your customers using your Apple device. With Tap to Pay, you no longer need to wait for customers to pay through digital links on invoices. Instead, payments are processed instantly as your customers tap their mobile device or card on your Apple device.

This new feature gives you a simpler, faster, and more secure payment method, streamlining how you receive payments. It makes getting paid quicker and easier, reducing admin hassle and improving your cash flow. Tap to Pay helps you focus more on work and less on chasing payments, giving you a smoother experience from both a business and admin standpoint.

Note:

At this time, Tap-to-Pay is only supported on Apple devices. You must also have set up hipages Payments to use this feature.

Enabling Tap to Pay

  1. Tap to Pay can be enabled in the following methods:

    • Selecting the Tap-to-Pay message on the To Do screen.

    • Selecting Enable Tap to Pay on iPhone in the Digital payments section of an invoice.

      The Enable Tap to Pay on iPhone screen is then displayed.

    • Selecting Profile → My Business → Quotes & Invoices Settings, and enabling Tap to Pay on iPhone.

  2. Select Enable Tap to Pay on iPhone.

    Tap to pay is then enabled on your Apple device.

  3. To accept a payment from your customer, select Tap to pay on iPhone in the Payments methods section of your invoice and hold out your device to your customer.

  4. Your customer will have to tap their preferred payment method on your device. Once payment has been successfully processed the following confirmation message will be displayed and you will be shown the Job Details screen.

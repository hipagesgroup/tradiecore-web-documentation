Setting up a new Individual / Sole trader business

If you do not have a Stripe account or you want to set up a new Stripe business, follow the steps listed here.

Note:

The information you are asked to provide during this process is required by law to verify your identity and business. To become a Stripe customer, the Australian government requires Stripe to collect information known as the Know Your Customer process. This is a legal requirement, not a hipages tradiecore policy.

  1. Go to the Tell us about your business screen.

    This screen is shown if you do not have a Stripe account, after entering the Stripe verification code, or if you choose the Create a New Business option during the Connecting to hipages Payments process.

  2. Using the Type of business option, specify what type your business is, as per your ABN Entity Type. The available options are:

    • Company

    • Partnership

    • Trust

    • Individual / Sole Trader

    • Not-for-profit (NFP)

  3. Choose the Individual / Sole Trader option.

    Note:

    Only select the Company option if you have a board of directors or governing board as part of your business. For more information on setting up a new business as a company with Stripe see Setting up a new Stripe company.

  4. Choose the structure of your business, in the Business structure section. Your options are: 

    • I have an Australian business number (ABN)

    • I do not have an ABN

    Note:

    In order to set up a new stripe business as a sole trader you are required to have an ABN number.

  5. Select Continue. The Tell us more about your business screen is then displayed.

  6. Enter the following information for your business. Non-mandatory fields have been indicated with the Optional label.

    • Legal business name

    • Australian business number (ABN)

    • Business name (Optional)

    • Business address

    • Business phone number

    • Industry

    • Business website

  7. When you are done, select Continue.

    You will then be asked to verify your personal details. This section must be filled by you.

  8. Enter the following to verify your personal details.

    • Legal name

    • Email address

    • Date of birth

    • Home address

    • Phone number

  9. Select Continue to proceed.

  10. You will then be prompted to select your preferred payment method for receiving funds from your customers. You can choose to receive payments via direct debit to your bank account or to a debit card.

    If you select the Bank account option, enter your BSB number, Account number, and re-enter it again in Confirm account number.

    If you select the Debit card option, enter your Card number and Expiry date.

  11. Once you have completed entering your payment details, select Continue. You will then be prompted to review the information that you entered.

    If any information is missing, it will be marked in red as Incomplete. Select Edit and provide the required details.

  12. If the information you entered is correct, select Agree & Submit.

    You will then receive an email from Stripe confirming that you are ready to receive payments via Stripe.

    You can now send invoices and quotes with deposits to your customers and receive credit card payments.

Cancelling or Deleting a Job

A job can be either cancelled or deleted in hipages tradiecore. If you cancel a job, the job status changes to cancelled; all details related to the job will remain in hipages tradiecore. If you delete a job the job is removed from the jobs list and all information related to the job is removed from hipages tradiecore.

Cancelling a job

To cancel a job,

  1. Select the quick actions option for the job from a tile on the To Do screen, Jobs list, or from the opened job.

  2. Choose Cancelled or Mark job as cancelled.

Deleting a Job

To delete a job,

  1. Open the job from the Jobs list.

  2. Select the more actions option .

  3. Select Delete Job.

    Note:

    Jobs with sent quotes or invoices cannot be deleted.

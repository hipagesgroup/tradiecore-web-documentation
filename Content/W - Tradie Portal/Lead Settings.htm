Lead Settings

Lead Settings lets you manage your preferences related to the services that you offer consumers and the area that you provide services. In Lead Settings you can add, edit, and remove the categories related to the services that you provide your customers. You can also change the distance that you are willing to travel for jobs and add, edit, or delete specific postcodes that you prefer working in.

Note:

The leads shown to you on the Leads screen depend on the categories and areas that you specify here.

Turning on and off the Similar Categories and Surrounding Postcodes options gives you the ability to further control what leads are displayed for you.

  • Similar Categories: Turning on this option will display leads from categories that are similar to the ones that you have selected as your service preferences.

  • Surrounding Postcodes: This option is enabled by default and ensures that leads from neighbouring postcodes to the ones that you have selected are displayed. If you do not want to see leads from the surrounding postcodes, turn the option off. When you turn it off, the option is shown greyed out.

Changing area preferences

To change your area preferences,

  1. Scroll down your tradie profile and select, Areas I Cover in the Lead Settings section.

  2. Expand the Areas I Cover option to view and manage your area preferences.

  3. Adjust the slider to define your service area by setting the preferred maximum driving distance from your chosen postcode.

  4. To change your postcode select . In the Update Centre Postcode pop-up enter a new postcode as your centre location.

    Your service area is based on the maximum driving distance you selected and the centre postcode you provided.

  5. Select or clear the area checkboxes you want to service.

    If the checkbox next to a main suburb/region is selected, it means you have selected all the postcodes within that area/region as locations where you provide service to your customers.

  6. To choose specific postcodes expand each main suburb/region by selecting the icon. The suburb/region then expands to allow you to further refine the areas you prefer to cover.

  7. Select Save when you have finished updating your area preferences to save your selection.

Changing category preferences

To change your area preferences,

  1. Scroll down your tradie profile and select, My Categories in the Lead Settings section.

  2. Expand the My Categories option to view and manage your category preferences.

  3. Select or clear the Enable Smart Category Match option, to receive leads from categories that are closely related to your preferred categories. By default this option is enabled, if you do not want to receive leads from closely related categories, clear the checkbox.

  4. The My Categories section shows you all the categories that you currently work in. Remove categories by selecting the icon.

  5. To search your categories type in the category that you want in the search field.

  6. To add more categories, search for the category you want using the search field in the Available Categories section.

  7. The select the add icon to add a category.

  8. To further refine your preferred categories, expand each main category by selecting by selecting the arrow icon. The add the specific category that you want by selecting the add icon.

  9. Select Save when you have finished updating your area preferences to save your selection.

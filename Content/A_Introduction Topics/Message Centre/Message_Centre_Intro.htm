Message Centre in hipages tradiecore

Message Centre in hipages tradiecore streamlines communication with your customers as well as hipages hipages tradiecore Support. It gives you an instant and direct method of communicating with your customers and Support.

With Message Centre you have the benefit of,

  • having all your chats neatly organised in one place on your device

  • no more searching through scattered messages to find what you need

  • quick communication with support for help and resolving issues

  • creating message templates that cuts down on the time taken to type out a message

We recommend that you use it as much as possible. When you first reach out to a customer, the Message Centre is a great way to spark conversation and start to start to build trust.

Opening Message Centre

Message Centre can be accessed from any screen on hipages tradiecore. The Message Centre icon is shown on the top of each screen, giving you access to contact your customers or Support quickly.

To open message centre,

  • Select Message Centre located at the top of each screen.

    The Message Centre is displayed, listing all your customer and support conversations.

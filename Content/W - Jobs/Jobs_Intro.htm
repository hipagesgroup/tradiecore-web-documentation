Jobs

The home of all your admin, Jobs brings everything to one place. Each time an accepted lead comes in, a job is automatically created, with all the paperwork related to it in one place. Easily send quotes & invoices to a customer, manage your accounting, and create and keep track of your appointments for a job from a single location.

Use the Jobs Board feature for a clear, visual overview of all the jobs in your pipeline, helping you manage multiple jobs at different stages with ease. This layout gives you full visibility over your work flow, helping you stay organised and focused throughout each job’s life cycle.

Note:

For a job to be automatically created when a lead is accepted, you must be connected to hipages. However, if needed you can also create jobs manually.

Jobs List

Using the Jobs list, you can: 

  • Filter jobs by status to quickly view only the jobs you want to focus on.

  • Search for specific jobs by entering a customer name or job number in the search field.

  • Create quotes or invoices directly from the list without opening the full job details.

  • Update a job’s status.

Creating external jobs

External jobs are those you create manually, typically for jobs you've received through methods other than the Leads.

To add a job manually to hipages tradiecore, follow the steps given below:

  1. Select Create + on the Jobs list.

    The Create a new job pop-up will appear.

  2. For an existing customer

    1. Enter the customer’s name in the Customer name field.

      A list of your customers matching the name that you entered will be displayed.

    2. Select the customer that you want from the list.

      The other fields will auto-populate with the customer’s details.

    For a new customer

    1. Enter the customer's name in the Customer name field.

    2. Select the Create 'customer name' as a new customer option.

    3. Enter either the customer's Phone number or Email address in their respective fields.

  3. Once you have selected an existing customer or created a new customer, select Create Job.

    A new job is then created, and you’ll be redirected to the new job page.

  4. From here, you can add further details, such as the job location and description, and start sending quotes and invoices to the customer.

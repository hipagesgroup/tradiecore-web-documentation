Creating a quote

A quote is the price for the job that you have received from a customer, it should contain material and the labour cost for the job. You can send several quotes for a job via email to your customers. In a quote you can also choose if a customer is required to pay a deposit amount and what the amount is. When a customer approves the quote or pays the deposit amount, the status of the quote and the job will change to accepted.

To add a quote to a job, follow the steps given below,

  1. Create a new quote by selecting Add in the Quotes section of a job.

    This displays a new quote page. The quote will contain your customer's details and your tax preferences.

  2. Enter a quote description in the Description section.

  3. In the Items section of the quote select Add to add a new line item.

  4. Enter the Item name, item Description, Quantity, Price and if needed Tax rate. hipages tradiecore web will then calculate the total for the line item and display it.

    If you add an previously saved item, the item description, price, and tax amounts are automatically filled in. You only need to add the quantity.

    As you enter items to your quote, hipages tradiecore web will automatically calculate the subtotal for all the items, the corresponding GST amount, and the full total of the quote.

  5. If you want to ensure that your customer commits to your quote, you can specify a deposit amount payable by your customer prior to commencing the job.

    In the Deposit section of the quote you can enter either a deposit amount or deposit percentage. The corresponding deposit totals are automatically calculated and displayed.

  6. Select Send to email the quote to your customer or select Save to save the quote as a draft.

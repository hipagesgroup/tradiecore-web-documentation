Job Statuses

Job statuses help you track the progress of your work and understand where each job currently stands. They also allow you to filter your job list and organise tasks across key areas of the app and web, such as the To Do screen and the Jobs Board.

Each job moves through a defined set of statuses as it progresses—from initial lead through to completion and payment. The available statuses are:

  • New

    Jobs that are newly created—either from accepted leads or manually added. No quotes or appointments have been issued yet.

  • Quoted

    Jobs for which you have sent one or more quotes to the customer, but haven’t been hired yet.

  • Hired

    Jobs where the customer has accepted your quote and confirmed that they want you to do the work.

  • Scheduled

    Jobs that have at least one appointment booked in the Schedule. This indicates that work has been planned or is underway.

  • Work Completed

    Jobs where the physical work has been completed, but you may still need to send an invoice or collect payment.

  • Invoiced

    Jobs for which you’ve issued an invoice, but payment is still pending.

  • Done

    Jobs that are fully completed and paid for. No further action is required.

  • Cancelled

    Jobs that have been cancelled by either you or the customer before completion.

Each status helps you stay organised and focused on what needs attention next. You can use filters to quickly find jobs in a specific stage and take the next steps as needed.

Manually updating a job status

In some cases, you may need to update a job’s status manually—for example, if a job is completed outside of the app or a customer confirms details over the phone.

To change the status of a job:

  1. Open the job.

  2. Tap the more options menu .

  3. Then choose the appropriate status from the list.

    This allows you to keep your records accurate and your job list up to date.

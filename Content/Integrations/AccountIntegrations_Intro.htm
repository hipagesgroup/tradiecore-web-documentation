Integrations

Tradiecore Integrations is where you set up connections to hipages, hipages Payments and your accounting software. By connecting to hipages, hipages customers will see their successfully accepted job leads pass seamlessly into Tradiecore, so all jobs can be managed in the same place.

Your invoicing process will be streamlined to improve efficiency and reduce entering the same information across multiple platforms. Your customer information and invoice data will automatically be sent to your accounting software, giving you more accurate data and more time to focus on getting your jobs completed. Tradiecore is integrated with Xero, QuickBooks, MYOB, & hipages payments for automatic and seamless data transfer instead of manually entering information across several apps.

With hipages lead integration,

  • The details from accepted hipages’ leads will automatically pre-populate in jobs.

  • A contact is automatically added for you.

  • The hipages icon will be on the job so you can identify where the job has come from.

Note:

To connect your Tradiecore account to hipages you require a hipages subscription. Register for a hipages account by selecting the link: hipages Tradie Registration. Once connected any leads you successfully accept will come into Tradiecore as a job for you to manage.

By connecting Tradiecore to an accounting tool,

  • You can automatically send your Tradiecore invoices to the accounting software of your choice for more streamlined invoicing and accounting accuracy.

  • You will no longer have to manually handle invoices or enter customer and invoice data.

  • With increased accuracy and up-to-date financial records, you can easily manage your cash flow and make more informed business decisions.

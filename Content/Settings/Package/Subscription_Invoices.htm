Viewing and paying your subscription invoices

To view your subscription invoices, follow these steps,

  1. Select Profile → My Package.

  2. On the My Package screen, select Package Billing.

    You will see the Package Billing screen showing you the option of viewing the invoices you have received from hipages or updating your credit card information.

  3. Select Package Invoices.

    The package Invoices screen is displayed, showing you all hipages subscription invoices and the status of each invoice. The invoice statuses are: 

    • Paid - Green

    • Outstanding - Red

    • Partially Paid - Yellow

  4. To view an invoice, select View invoice.

Paying your subscription invoices

To pay a subscription invoice in hipages tradiecore,

  1. To pay an invoice select Pay now.

    The payment selection screen is displayed, showing you two payment methods.

  2. If you want to pay with a card that you have saved for hipages tradiecore, select Pay with existing card.

  3. To pay with a different card, select Pay with new card.

  4. Enter the details for the new credit card and select Add Credit Card.

  5. Select Pay now.

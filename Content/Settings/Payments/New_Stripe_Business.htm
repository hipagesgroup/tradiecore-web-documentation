Setting up a new Stripe business

If you do not have a Stripe account or you want to set up a new Stripe business, follow the steps listed here.

  1. Navigate to the Tell us about your business screen. This screen is displayed if you do not have a Stripe account and you entered the Stripe verification code or if you select the Create a new business option when Connecting to hipages Payments.

  2. Using the Type of business option, choose what type your business is as per your ABN Entity Type. The available options are:

    • Company

    • Partnership

    • Trust

    • Individual / Sole Trader

    • Not-for-profit (NFP)

  3. Choose the structure of your business, using the Business structure option. You can choose if your company is a Proprietary company or if it is a Public company.

  4. Select Continue. The Tell us more about your business screen will be displayed.

  5. Enter the following information for your business. Non-mandatory fields have been indicated with the Optional label.

    • Legal business name

    • Australian business number (ABN)

    • Australian company number (ACN)

    • Doing business as

    • Registered business address

    • Business phone number

    • Industry

    • Business website

  6. When you are done, select Continue.

    You will then be asked to verify your management of the company. This section can be filled by you or by someone else who has significant control or management of your company.

  7. Enter the following to verify the business.

    • Legal name

    • Email address

    • Job title

    • Date of birth

    • Home address

    • Phone number

  8. Then choose your level of ownership or management within the company by selecting either, I own 25% of the company or I am a member of the governing board of the company options.

  9. Enter the percentage of the business you have ownership of in the Percent ownership of your business.

  10. Select Continue. The Business Directors screen will be displayed.

  11. Select Add a director to enter details about the directors in your company or select Add directors later to enter the information later.

  12. Then select Continue. You will then be prompted to enter information about the executives of your company.

  13. Select Done, continue to proceed.

  14. Enter your bank details next. Your BSB number and Account number is required.

  15. Select Save. You will then have to review the information that you entered.

  16. If the information you entered is correct, select Agree & Submit.

    You will then receive an email from Stripe confirming that you are ready to receive payments via Stripe.

    You can now send invoices and quotes with deposits to your customers and receive credit card payments.

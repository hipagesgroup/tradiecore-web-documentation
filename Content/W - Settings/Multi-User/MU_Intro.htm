Multi User in hipages tradiecore

Coming soon!

The multi-user feature on hipages tradiecore web allows you to efficiently manage your business by adding new users directly to your profile. This self-service functionality eliminates the need to contact support, giving you greater control over your account and team. For businesses with employees, this feature also enables you to assign roles, distribute jobs, and manage leads, streamlining work-flows and improving overall job management.

Adding a new team member

To add a new team member to your profile you must be the account/business owner of the business. Follow the steps given here to add a new team member:

  1. Select Settings → My Team → Add new user .

    You will see the Add new user screen.

  2. Enter your new team member's information including Name, Mobile Number, and Email.

    On this page, you’ll also see the role assigned to your new team member and the permissions they will have.

  3. Once the new team member's information has been added, select Send invitation .

    You will then see the following confirmation message, and your new team member will receive an email with an invitation link to set-up their account as a team member in hipages tradiecore.

    Your My team page will also be updated to show your new team member with their status as Pending.

Note:

A new team member will have 48 hours to accept and set up their account with hipages tradiecore. The invitation link expires after 48 hours and the individual will no longer be able to accept the invite and set up their account.
